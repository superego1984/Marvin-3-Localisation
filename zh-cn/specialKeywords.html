Marvin will find books searching for titles, authors, series, notes, and subjects.

You can also use any one of the following special keywords in the search box for advanced filtering. Special searches start with a keyword, followed by an optional search string:

<space>r

  • A space followed by an ‘r’ shows books that are marked as read.

Example 1:
‘<space>r<space>brave new world’ searches for the text ‘brave new world’ in your read books.

Example 2:
‘<space>r’ by itself only shows read books.

<space>u

  • A space followed by a ‘u’ shows unread books only.

Example 1:
‘<space>u<space>tolkien’ searches for the text ‘tolkien’ in your unread books.

Example 2:
‘<space>u’ by itself only shows unread books.

<space>n

  • A space followed by an ‘n’ shows books that are marked with the new flag.

<space>l

  • A space followed by an ‘l’ shows books in your reading list.

<space>t

  • A space followed by a ‘t’ shows books having a note.

<space>e

  • A space followed by an ‘e’ shows EPUB books only.

<space>c

  • A space followed by a ‘c’ shows CBR and CBZ comic books only.

<space>1

  • A space followed by a ‘1’ shows books that are rated with one star.

<space>2

  • A space followed by a ‘2’ shows books that are rated with two stars.

<space>3

  • A space followed by a ‘3’ shows books that are rated with three stars.

<space>4

  • A space followed by a ‘4’ shows books that are rated with four stars.

<space>5

  • A space followed by a ‘5’ shows books that are rated with five stars.

<space>3.

  • A space followed by a ‘3’ and a ‘.’ shows books that are rated three stars or better.

<space>3-

  • A space followed by a ‘3’ and a ‘-’ shows books that are rated less than three stars.

<space>.

  • A space followed by a ‘.’ shows books whose word count is less than 50,000 words.

<space>..

  • A space followed by two ‘.’s shows books whose word count is between 50,000 and 120,000 words.

<space>...

  • A space followed by three ‘.’s shows books whose word count is between 120,000 and 200,000 words.

<space>....

  • A space followed by four ‘.’s shows books whose word count is more than 120,000 words.

<space>x

  • A space followed by an ‘x’ shows unrated books only.
