This is the template that Marvin will use when sharing using social networks. You can use placeholders in your template. They aren’t case-senstive and include:

{title}

{title} will be replaced with the title of the book.

{author}

{author} will be replaced with the author(s) of the book.

{progress}

{progress} will be replaced with your percentage progress in the book including the percentage sign. E.g. ‘24%’.
