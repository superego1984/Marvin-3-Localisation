CBx Settings

These are the settings applicable when you open comics in the CBR and CBZ formats.

Global

  • Fast theme switching: enables tapping with two fingers anywhere on the page to cycle through your reading color themes (paper colors). This is useful, if you want to quickly switch themes without opening any menus.
  • Multi-color bookmarks: bookmarks in Marvin are colored red. If you enable this, you’ll be able to create bookmarks with different colors (red, green, or blue). You might use differently colored bookmarks to represent different kinds of annotations.
  • Bottom progress bar: shows a thin progress bar at the bottom of the screen.
  • Show zoom widget: shows a widget that lets you zoom in and out of pages without needing to pinch.
  • Framing size: This is the size of the page when zoomed to fit the screen. Choose a value less than 100% if you like having a matte border around the page.
  • Page turn style: the kind of animation Marvin should use when turning pages.
  • Animations: the speed of animations when turning pages and ‘zig-zagging’.
  • Gestures: determines what page taps and swipes do (e.g. tapping on the right to move to the next page).
