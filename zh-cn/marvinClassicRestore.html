Marvin is expecting a Marvin Classic backup file called ‘marvin.backup’ in your documents directory.

Migrating from Marvin Classic

This guide will help you migrate from Marvin Classic (version 2.x) to Marvin 3.

Go to Marvin Classic:

1. In Marvin Classic, tap the settings button.

2. Switch to the ‘Services’ tab.

3. Slide to the ‘Backup’ page and tap ‘Backup now’.

4. When your backup is ready choose ‘Send’.

5. Choose this version of Marvin from the list to send the backup to.

Back to Marvin here:

1. Tap the settings button and choose ‘General Settings’.

2. Scroll down to and choose ‘Backup and restore’.

3. Choose ‘Restore from Marvin Classic’ and follow the on-screen instructions.
