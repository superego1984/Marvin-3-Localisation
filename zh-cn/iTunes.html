Adding books and comics from your computer using iTunes

1. Connect your device to your computer

2. Launch iTunes

3. Select your device

Locate and select your device in iTunes:

4. Find Marvin file sharing

Find Marvin in the ‘Apps List’, and drag your EPUB and CBx books and comics to the file sharing panel.

5. Wait for the copy to complete

When Marvin detects new books being copied, you’ll see a popup asking you whether you’d like to import your new books. Please wait for the iTunes transfer to complete before starting the import.

Enjoy your new books!
